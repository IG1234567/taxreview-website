Available Tax Reliefs for Individuals

  • Personal Allowance: Tax-free income up to a certain threshold.
  • Charitable Donations: Tax deductions for donations to registered charities.
  • Gift Exemptions: Certain gifts are not subject to taxation.
  • Dividend Allowance: A portion of dividend income is tax-free.
  • Non-Taxable Income: Inheritances, compensation payments, and certain government benefits.
  • Child Tax Credit: Tax relief for families with dependent children.
  • Education Expenses: Deductions for tuition, student loan interest, and scholarships.
  • Medical Expense Deduction: Eligible out-of-pocket medical costs may be deducted.
  • Retirement Contributions: Tax relief on contributions to pension and retirement plans.
  • Home Mortgage Interest Deduction: Interest on qualifying home loans may be deductible.
  • Rental Income Exemptions: Certain rental earnings under a threshold may be tax-free.
  • Capital Gains Tax Relief: Relief for selling personal assets or investments.
  • Disability Tax Credit: Relief for individuals with disabilities or their caregivers.
  • Tax-Free Savings Accounts: Interest earned on certain savings accounts may be tax-free.
  • Foreign Income Exemption: Certain earnings from abroad may qualify for exclusions.
  • Transportation Benefits: Relief for work-related transportation expenses.
